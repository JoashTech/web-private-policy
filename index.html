Privacy Policy for World English Bible

Last Updated: August 21, 2025

JoshTechApps ("we," "us," or "our") develops World English Bible ("the App"), a free, ad-supported application providing daily Bible verses, prayers, quizzes, and customization features. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use the App.

Important Note About Advertising and Analytics: Our app uses Google AdMob for advertising and Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics for usage analytics and crash reporting. Please review AdMob's Privacy Policy, Firebase Privacy Information, and Google's Privacy Policy for detailed information about how they handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display ads (e.g., Interstitial, Native Advanced, Rewarded). AdMob may collect device identifiers such as Advertising ID, IP address, device type, and usage data to serve personalized ads, as described in Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob's Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized data about app usage, such as screen views, button clicks, and session duration. This data helps us improve the App’s functionality and user experience, as outlined in Firebase Privacy Information and Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Crash Reporting Data: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic information to improve the App’s stability. This may include device information, stack traces, and custom logs, as described in Firebase Privacy Information and Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Local Data: We store notes, favorite verses, and quiz progress locally on your device using Room persistence. This data is not transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.
  • Notifications: If you enable notifications in the App's settings, we use device permissions to schedule daily reminders for verses and prayers at 08:00 and 20:00.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect personal details such as your name, email, or location unless you voluntarily provide them (e.g., via email contact).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Advertising: AdMob uses collected data to serve relevant ads.
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics data helps us understand user interactions to enhance the App’s features and performance.
  • Crash Reporting: Firebase Crashlytics data is used to identify and fix app crashes and improve stability.
  • Local Data: Notes, favorite verses, and quiz progress are used to provide personalized features within the App.
  • Notifications: We use notification permissions to deliver daily reminders as configured by you.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your local data or notification preferences with any third parties. AdMob may share device identifiers with its advertising partners (e.g., ad networks) to serve ads, and Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics may share anonymized usage and crash data with Google, as outlined in Google's Privacy Policy, AdMob's Privacy Policy, and Firebase Privacy Information. We do not use other third-party SDKs that collect data.

4. Your Choices

You have the following options regarding your data:

  • Opt out of personalized ads by resetting your Advertising ID or disabling personalized ads in your device's settings (e.g., Android Settings > Google > Ads).
  • Disable Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics data collection by adjusting your device’s settings or contacting us for assistance, as described in Firebase Privacy Information.
  • Disable notifications in the App's settings or your device's notification settings.
  • Delete local data by clearing the App's data through your device's settings or uninstalling the App.
  • Learn more about AdMob's data practices and controls in their AdMob's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain local data (notes, favorite verses, quiz progress) on your device until you clear the App's data or uninstall it. We do not store any personal data on our servers. Data collected by AdMob, Firebase Analytics, and Crashlytics is retained according to Google’s policies, as described in Google's Privacy Policy and Firebase Privacy Information.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is rated E for Everyone and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13 years of age, please do not submit any Personal Information through our Mobile Application. We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's internet usage and to help us enforce this policy by instructing their children never to provide Personal Information through the Mobile Application. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it immediately. Please contact us if you have concerns.

7. Data Security

We rely on Android's built-in security features, such as app sandboxing, to protect local data stored on your device. Since no data is transmitted to our servers, there is no risk of server-side data breaches.

8. International Compliance

For users in the European Union (GDPR) or California (CCPA), you may have additional rights, such as requesting data access or deletion. Since we only store data locally and use AdMob, Firebase Analytics, and Crashlytics for ads and analytics, you can exercise these rights through your device settings or Google's ad and analytics settings, as described in Firebase Privacy Information. Contact us for assistance.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post the updated policy on this page, and your continued use of the App constitutes consent to those changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contactjoshtech@gmail.com.
